About

I am a hard working professional living in the Portland area. I am originally from Phoenix and have been in the Portland area since 2018. I have been working in the IT field for nearly a decade, and am excited to make the switch over to programming.

After working my way up from Junior IT Technician to Network and Systems Administrator, I found myself with the opportunity to travel the world. Over the course of April 2016 to April 2018, I travelled to over 40 different countries, seeing sites such as the Great Wall of China, The Great Ocean Road, and Victoria Falls.

My travels eventually lead me to a job working as a Help Desk Specialist at a ski resort in Australia. Once the season was over, I returned back to Phoenix to work temporarily again for my former company as an IT Contractor.
