About

I am a hard working professional living in the Portland area. I am originally from Phoenix and have been in the Portland area since 2018. I have been working in the IT field for nearly a decade, and am excited to make the switch over to programming.

After working my way up from Junior IT Technician to Network and Systems Administrator, I found myself with the opportunity to travel the world. Over the course of April 2016 to April 2018, I travelled to over 40 different countries, seeing sites such as the Great Wall of China, The Great Ocean Road, and Victoria Falls.

My travels eventually lead me to a job working as a Help Desk Specialist at a ski resort in Australia. Once the season was over, I returned back to Phoenix to work temporarily again for my former company as an IT Contractor.

From there, I moved to Portland and began working at an MSP CORE Business Services (now Kelley Connect). I started as a Helpdesk Technician, and was quickly promoted to the job Network Engineer.

My most recent job was at Unitus Community Credit Union, where I started as a Helpdesk Specialist and was first promoted to Systems Administrator, then Senior Helpdesk Specialist.

Edit Sub-Calendar Permissions

This script enables Office 365 admins to add delegate permissions to user's calendars, sub-calendars, and Contacts.

I wrote this script as it was frustrating to have to get in contact with executives when they wanted a new assistant to have access to a specific sub-calendar. This also ended up being useful for permissions on rooms/equipment.

Interface built with WPF, code in PowerShell.

Remote Assist Over RDP

This script allows you to query a domain computer, get the active sessions on the machine, and start a shadow session with the user utilizing RDP (mstsc.exe). You are able to remote assist users that are connected to their PCs with RDP.

Interface built with WPF, code in PowerShell.
